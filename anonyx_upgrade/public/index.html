What Is Anonymous Chat?

Anonymous chat lets you talk to strangers online without sharing your real identity. People use it for casual conversations, passing time, finding interesting people, or just escaping noisy social apps.

Anonyx keeps the experience light and direct while adding practical upgrades like safety controls, better matching, and a cleaner interface.
